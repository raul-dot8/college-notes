## 1
When you adding or subtracting values with a known uncertainty, add the uncertainties together.
For example:
$$
198\pm 0.5mm\text{ }-\text{ }60\pm 0.5mm=138\pm 1mm
$$
## 2
When multiplying values with uncertainties, convert the uncertainties into percentages of the original value, and add them.
$$
\displaylines{
0.14940\text{ [kg]} \cdot 9.81 \cdot 0.31\text{ [m]}=0.454\text{ [Nm]}\\
\frac{0.1494\text{ [kg]}}{\pm0.0001\text{ [kg]}}\cdot100 = \pm 0.067\% \\
\frac{0.31\text{ [m]}}{\pm 0.01\text{ [m]}}=\pm 3.2\% \\
0.067+3.2=\pm3.3\% \\
0.454\pm 3.3\% \text{ [Nm]}
}
$$
