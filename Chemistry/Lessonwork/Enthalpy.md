# Endothermic and exothermic
In a reaction, bonds must break before they can be made. Bond breaking is endothermic process as it takes energy to break the bonds. Making bonds is an exothermic process as energy is released to the surroundings.
# What is enthalpy?
Enthalpy is the measure of the total energy of a system.[^1] We cannot directly measure it, as it is the energy stored in a system. Such, we measure the enthalpy changes in a system.
